Rent mechanic

Simple, transparent, pay-per-use renting.

A clear journey from “I need a tractor tomorrow morning” to “I’ve paid and rated the work” – for both farmer and owner.

Farmer side

Step-by-step rental flow

  1. Discover: Enter crop, work type and date to see nearby equipment and rates.
  2. Select: Choose a tractor/implement based on price, ratings and distance.
  3. Schedule: Pick time slot and hours needed; see total rental cost upfront.
  4. Confirm: Lock the booking with a small advance payment.
  5. Complete & Rate: Work is done, remaining amount is paid, both parties rate each other.
Owner side

From idle asset to steady income

  1. List: Add machine details, photos, base hourly rate and service area.
  2. Manage slots: Block personal-use days, keep the rest open for rent.
  3. Accept: Review booking request and confirm jobs that fit your schedule.
  4. Track & work: Use GPS guidance for navigation and on-time arrival.
  5. Get paid: Receive digital payments and view earnings in a simple dashboard.
Pricing

Fair, visible rates

Hourly and per-acre pricing is shown before booking, with additional charges (fuel, transport) clearly broken down.

Trust

Ratings & reviews

Every job ends with a rating from both sides, building a trusted pool of reliable owners and serious farmers.

Transparency

Digital trail

Each booking has a job history – who rented, when, for how long and how much was paid – reducing disputes.
